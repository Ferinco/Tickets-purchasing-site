Moon

The Moon is a natural satellite of Earth and the fifth largest moon in the solar system. It has a diameter of about one-quarter that of Earth and is the only celestial object that humans can see with the naked eye that orbits around our planet. The Moon's phases, tides, and effects on Earth's atmosphere and wildlife make it an important object of scientific study.
